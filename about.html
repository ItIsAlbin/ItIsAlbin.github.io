About

I'm a free-time indie game developer and currently studing to become a web developer at Yrgo, Göteborg. My background is in art and design, the latter in which I also hold an univeristy bachelor degree.

I've been working with 3d-software, mostly Autodesk Maya, for about 15 years and Unity the last 8+ years, learning C# as I go along. Alongside this I also enjoy drawing/painting, be it digital or on paper.

My main focus has usually been 3d modeling, although I also enjoy doing animation from time to time. Topics tends to lean towards architecture (cities in general), people and some occasional sci-fi.

I also play and write music, enjoy learning languages and hang out with my part time dog.
