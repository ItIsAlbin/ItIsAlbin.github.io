Future Mage: Spellmaker

A procedural top-down action game where you program your own spells through an in-game node editor. Being able to buy more advanced nodes as you progress through the levels.

The game was made in Unity, while all characters, game props and animations was made in Maya.

Everything was created by me except the sounds.
